Our Services

At Clayway, we believe that communication is the cornerstone of any successful enterprise. That's why we offer a range of services to help our clients tell their story, connect with their audience, and achieve their goals. Whether you need help with public relations, marketing, advertising, or digital media strategy, we have the skills and experience to deliver results.

What sets us apart is our approach. We take the time to understand our clients' unique needs and goals, and we develop customized solutions to help them achieve success. We're a small, nimble team that's committed to building strong relationships with our clients, and we're always available to offer advice, answer questions, and provide support.

Media Monitoring

Our TV Media Monitoring Unit is an essential service for ensuring the accuracy and timeliness of advertising releases. With the proliferation of TV channels, media monitoring has become increasingly important.

Our unit cross-checks ads to ensure that they are broadcasted according to schedule, and we maintain a detailed record of all TVC releases in our database.
